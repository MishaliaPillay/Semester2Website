Space, why so scary ?

Space data is scary and very overwhelming. My goal for this Data Driven Artwork was to entice the emotion of ease when looking at this data set. I wanted to make a data visualization that was inviting and easy to understand, I wanted this Data Driven Artwork to cater for various types of users such as those who are and are not familiar with Asteroid data. For this semester I have worked very closely with the NASA API for Near Earth Objects, more specifically, Asteroids. I have felt overwhelmed and unsure that I would be able to understand the data, however, the more time I spent trying to understand it, the more interesting I found it to be. I wanted to share this with any user that comes across this site, and hopefully help them gain an understanding of Asteroid data, without overloading them with too much scientific information that requires an area of expertise to unpack. My simple goal was to accommodate the user and invite them to a colorful ,fun way of understanding and displaying Asteroid data.
